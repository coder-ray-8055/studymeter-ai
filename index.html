About StudyMeter

StudyMeter is an AI-powered web application designed to help students estimate their exam performance based on their weekly study hours. By using a Machine Learning model, the platform analyzes the relationship between study time and marks, providing users with quick and easy predictions. This tool is built to encourage better study habits, improve consistency, and give students a rough idea of where they stand before exams. StudyMeter is simple, fast, and free to use — no sign-up required. Just enter your study hours and get instant predictions. Note: The predictions are based on a simplified model and may not reflect exact real-world results, as many other factors can influence performance.

Although we predict your marks, your actual results depend on your hard work. Hard work is the key to success.
